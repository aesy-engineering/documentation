## Create your first recommendation quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Root Shopify app & Open it">
    Open the app to land on your Root dashboard
    <img
      className="block dark:hidden"
      src="/images/root-dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Light"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/root-dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Shop from the side bar">
    You should see your shopify store on this page. Click on the shop card and navigate to the inner page, to find sync buttons
    <img
      className="block dark:hidden"
      src="/images/shop-list.png"
      alt="Hero Light"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/shop-list.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync products from your store">
    Click 'Sync Products' button to your products from your shop to Root platform
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync the products to AI">
    Click 'Sync AI' button to make our AI learn about your products.
    <Tip>Once AI sync is complete the  button should show 0 more. If not try syncing again, till it shows zero more</Tip>
    <img
      className="block dark:hidden"
      src="/images/shop-sync.png"
      alt="Hero Light"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/shop-sync.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to recommendations page">
    Click on recommendations tab from the side-bar.
    The recommendations page is where you can find all your products reccomendations. Click on `Create new recommendation` to get started
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask AI to Generate a quiz for you">
    1. Choose the shop from the drop down
    2. Enter a prompt to help AI understand what kind of quiz you want to create. eg "Fetch the toys that are best for 10-year and create a quiz for them to choose the right product for them "
    3. Press "Generate new recommendation", and wait for the recommendation graph to appear
    4. Use the fine tuning prompt to fine tune your quiz with the help of AI. eg: "Change the question which asks about budget and add a question about color preference"
    6. Double click on the graph nodes to edit the questions or options
    7. Double click on recommendation node, to edit the recommendation. AI generated recommendations work neverthless
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize and create the quiz">
    Review and create
    1. Give a `name` to the quiz & a good background image
    2. Give a great `title` that will appear in the front page of your quiz eg: "Find the right toy for you !"
    3. Change the theme by changing the primary, secondary & background color, to fit your brands identity
    4. Press `Create`. You should see the quiz created and a preview appears on the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add the quiz to your shop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the shopify admin panel">
    Go to shopify admin panel for your store, and click on `Online Store > Themes` > `Customize`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the page and add app block">
    Navigate to the page where you wanna add the quiz & add the product Recommendation app block by Root, by using `+ Add Block`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the BusinessId & RecommendationId">
    On the right side pannel, you will be asked to add `BusinessId` & `RecommendationId`, copy the same from the quiz page.
    <img
      className="block dark:hidden"
      src="/images/copy-sample.png"
      alt="Hero Light"
    />
    <img
      className="hidden dark:block"
      src="/images/copy-sample.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the changes and check your shop website to see the quiz in action
  </Step>
</Steps>
